Sharlet Lifestyle Information

About Sharlet Lifestyle

Sharlet Lifestyle was founded with the vision of offering stylish,
affordable, and carefully curated fashion.
Our focus is on pieces that feel good to wear and are easy to style,
allowing you to express yourself confidently.
We believe fashion should feel personal, not overwhelming.
That’s why we prioritize quality selections, honest communication, and a customer-first experience.

Returns and Refunds

At Sharlet Lifestyle, all sales are final. Due to the nature of our business,
we do not accept returns or offer refunds once an item has been purchased.
We kindly ask customers to review item details carefully before placing an order.
We are always happy to provide additional information, photos, or measurements
upon request to help you make an informed decision before purchasing.

How to Order

Placing an order with Sharlet Lifestyle is simple:
1.Browse our available items on our social media platforms or website
2. Select the item you wish to purchase
3. Contact us via WhatsApp or direct mSessage to confirm availability
4. Complete payment as guided
Your order will be prepared and dispatched accordingly. If you need help before ordering, feel free to reach out, we’re happy to assist.
